About Our Initiative

Pakistan generates millions of tons of waste annually, causing severe environmental and health issues. Our initiative aims to create a sustainable waste management system to reduce pollution and promote recycling.

We work with local authorities, NGOs, and communities to develop effective waste collection, segregation, and disposal strategies, ensuring a cleaner and greener Pakistan.
